Custom PC Building

Every PC I build is fully custom-made, designed specifically for your needs—no generic pre-built systems. Whether you’re a first-time buyer or a tech enthusiast, I’ll guide you through the process to ensure you get the best possible setup.

For Beginners

Not sure what you need? No problem! You’ll simply check a box indicating that this is your first PC, and I’ll guide you step by step. You’ll answer a few simple questions like:

  • What will you use the PC for? (e.g., gaming, work, video editing, general use)
  • What’s your budget?

From there, I’ll recommend the best possible build for your needs and explain everything in clear, easy-to-understand terms. No overwhelming tech jargon—just honest advice.

For Experienced Users

If you already know what you want, you can select your preferred components directly, including:

  • GPU
  • CPU
  • RAM
  • Storage
  • Case style
  • Overall price range

Once submitted, I’ll review your selections and fine-tune the build to ensure compatibility, performance, and efficiency.

What's Included?

Every PC comes with:

  • A full setup and stress test before delivery
  • Optional software installation (upon request).
  • Direct support if you have any questions after your purchase

Your PC should work exactly how you need it to—and I make sure it does!

PC Upgrades

Whether you’re upgrading your PC for the first time or you already know exactly what you need, I offer a personalized approach to ensure you get the best performance for your budget.
