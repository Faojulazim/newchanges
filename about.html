About Me

I've been passionate about PC building and tech repairs since I was young. What started as a hobby quickly turned into something more when I realized how overpriced pre-built systems were. I wanted to offer affordable, high-quality, and customized solutions for people who need a reliable PC that fits their exact needs.

Unlike big retailers, I work closely with my customers to ensure they get a personalized experience. Whether you're new to PCs or a tech enthusiast, I'm here to provide expert guidance, honest pricing, and hands-on support for your system. Let’s build something great together!

My goal isn’t just to make money—it’s to make technology accessible and fair. I believe in honest pricing, clear communication, and giving people exactly what they need without unnecessary extras. If you're looking for a custom-built PC, an upgrade, or a small repair, I’d be happy to help!

Custom PC Building

Every PC I build is fully custom-made, designed specifically for your needs—no generic pre-built systems. Whether you’re a first-time buyer or a tech enthusiast, I’ll guide you through the process to ensure you get the best possible setup.

For Beginners

Not sure what you need? No problem! You’ll simply check a box indicating that this is your first PC, and I’ll guide you step by step. You’ll answer a few simple questions like:

  • What will you use the PC for? (e.g., gaming, work, video editing, general use)
  • What’s your budget?

From there, I’ll recommend the best possible build for your needs and explain everything in clear, easy-to-understand terms. No overwhelming tech jargon—just honest advice.

For Experienced Users

If you already know what you want, you can select your preferred components directly, including:

  • GPU
  • CPU
  • RAM
  • Storage
  • Case style
  • Overall price range

Once submitted, I’ll review your selections and fine-tune the build to ensure compatibility, performance, and efficiency.

What's Included?

Every PC comes with:

  • A full setup and stress test before delivery
  • Optional software installation (upon request).
  • Direct support if you have any questions after your purchase

Your PC should work exactly how you need it to—and I make sure it does!

Warranty & Support

I offer a two-year general warranty on every PC I build, ensuring that if anything goes wrong within that period, I’ll take care of it. However, I also go beyond that— since every component comes with its own manufacturer warranty, I make sure you get the best possible coverage. If a part like your RAM, CPU, or GPU has a warranty that extends beyond my two-year coverage—such as a b five-year manufacturer warranty—I will personally check with the manufacturer or retailer to see if it can still be repaired or replaced. My commitment doesn’t stop when my own warranty ends; I will always help you navigate the warranty process, troubleshoot issues, and ensure you get the most out of your system. Whether it’s within my warranty period or covered by the manufacturer, I’ll make sure you’re not left dealing with problems alone.

PC Upgrades

Whether you’re upgrading your PC for the first time or you already know exactly what you need, I offer a personalized approach to ensure you get the best performance for your budget.

For Beginners

If you’re unsure about what needs to be upgraded, I can analyze your system and help you figure it out. Whether you’re trying to run a specific game or software, I’ll check your current setup, identify potential bottlenecks, and recommend the best upgrade options. If something isn’t performing as it should, I’ll run tests and benchmarks to find the exact issue and suggest solutions that fit your needs.

For Experienced Users

If you already know what you want, you can simply tell me which parts you need, and I’ll let you know what pricing and availability I can offer. Whether it’s a GPU, CPU, RAM, or storage upgrade, I’ll ensure compatibility and provide installation if needed

My Process:

  • Personalized recommendations based on your needs and budget.
  • System analysis & benchmarking to identify bottlenecks.
  • Upgrade installation & testing to ensure everything works smoothly
  • Direct consultation no unnecessary upselling, just the right upgrade for you.

No matter your experience level, I take a personal look at your system and make sure you get exactly what you need—nothing more, nothing less
